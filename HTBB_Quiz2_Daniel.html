Daniel — Children's Ministry

The Scenario

Daniel recently began serving as a volunteer in the HTBB Children's Ministry.

During one Sunday session, several volunteers briefly step out to prepare materials for the next activity. Daniel realises that he is alone in the classroom with one child, Ted, while the other children have already moved to the next station.

Later that morning, Ted trips while playing and scrapes his knee. Daniel helps him up and reassures him. Ted begins to cry and asks for a hug.

After the session, Ted's parent thanks Daniel for helping during the class and asks if Daniel could send Ted encouraging messages during the week.

Daniel wants to be supportive but is unsure what the appropriate safeguarding practices are in these situations.
