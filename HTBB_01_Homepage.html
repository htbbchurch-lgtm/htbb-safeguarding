Welcome from HTBB

Hi, and welcome! 👋

We're so glad you're here serving with us at Holy Trinity Bukit Bintang. Whether you're volunteering with children's ministry, youth, or any team that works with vulnerable members of our community — this training is for you.

Safeguarding is not just a formality. It reflects our commitment to making HTBB a safe church, where every person is protected, valued, and cared for. This course will equip you with the knowledge and confidence to serve safely and respond well.

This training is mandatory for all HTBB volunteers working with minors and vulnerable adults.
